Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Premoney?

    Premoney is an Instant Personal Loan Platform for individuals, where you can apply for a Loan of up to ₹ 30,000. Premoney acts as a technology intermediary and facilitates this Personal Loans from regulated lenders to individuals. The documentation is very minimal. The application process is completely online, and on approval, the cash is immediately transferred to the bank account of the user.

  • Who can apply loan in premoney?

    Any individual aged between 18 – 65 years having a monthly income of minimum Rs.15,000 through any authentic source, can apply for a loan with Premoney. We don’t discriminate between salaried or self-employed applicants, all are welcome as long as minimum income criteria along with other conditions is met.

  • How does Premoney works?

    Premoney is a technology platform which helps individuals avail Personal Loan through regulated lending partners. Applying for a Personal Loan through the Premoney Platform is simple, quick and easy. Just follow the below steps:

    1. Apply for loan through webform
    2. Fill in your basic details to check your eligibility for availing a Personal Loan through Premoney. This process hardly takes 15-20min, after which you shall be shown an ‘eligibility successful’ page. An SMS indicating the same shall also be sent over to your registered mobile number (Sometimes, this process might take up to 24 hours in case there is a detailed check that is required. In such cases, you shall receive a call from Premoney within 1 working day of application).
    3. Upon confirmation on the eligibility, you will need to upload your KYC documents and fill in a few more details about yourself. It should not take you more than 5-10 minutes to do the same.
    4. Once you have uploaded the KYC documents and the other additional information, the profile will be verified depending on the authenticity and quality of the documents uploaded.
    5. Once verified, you can apply for a loan through Premoney and sign a Loan agreement. After the Loan agreement is signed and loan application is created, the amount shall be disbursed into the bank account provided by you within 15 minutes.

  • On what basis Premoney Lend?

    Premoney has tied up with Unnati Microfin Private Limited, which is certified NBFC as lending Partner.

  • What are the documents I submit for the personal loan?

    Each user who applies for a personal loan will be required to submit two kinds of document:

    1. PAN: We collect your PAN details to check the financial parameters.
    2. Proof of Address and Identity: You may provide us with a copy of your Aadhaar or Masked e-Aadhaar or Passport or Voter ID.

    Each user must mandatorily submit their PAN card along with one of the documents mentioned above as Identity and Address Proof. Please note that submission of Aadhar is voluntary and you may submit any one of the other alternatives provided above.

  • Where is the loan disbursed?

    Premoney has tied up with Unnati Microfin Private Limited, which is certified NBFC as lending Partner.

  • I Have Never Taken A Loan Before, Can I Apply For A Loan With Premoney?

    Yes, at Premoney, we welcome first time borrowers.

  • Will my credit limit change under any circumstances?

    Yes. It can increase if you pay your EMIs and bills on time. There is a very good chance that our partner banks will raise your credit limit provided you have not taken too many other loans or seen a significant reduction in your income.

  • Do I need to open a Savings/Current account with Partner Bank for using Premoney?

    It is not compulsory to open an account in the bank.
